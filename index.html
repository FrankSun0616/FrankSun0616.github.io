Research Experience

Pioneering work at the intersection of physics, machine learning, and computational astrophysics

Neural Network Application for Jet Calibration

January 2024 - Current • Advisor: Jennifer Roloff
Developing advanced Machine Learning models for jet calibration using Particle Flow Networks (PFN). Implemented innovative dual-stream framework and knowledge distillation algorithms, achieving significant improvements in calibration accuracy with minimal error rates.
Neural Networks Particle Physics Python

Optical Galaxy Redshift Survey

October 2023 - Current • Tianlai Collaboration
Conducting comprehensive redshift survey in the North Celestial Cap, successfully obtaining 724 redshifts from 2801 targets. Utilizing advanced V-band photometry and spectroscopic observations with the WIYN telescope to support hydrogen intensity mapping studies.
Observational Astronomy Data Analysis Spectroscopy

Stellar Evolution & Nucleosynthesis

January 2022 - September 2022 • Advisor: Christopher West
Applied machine learning algorithms (Random Forest) with Grid Search optimization to develop best-fit isotopic scaling models. Achieved remarkable consistency with less than 8.7% error, leading to significant modifications in chemical evolution models.
Machine Learning MESA Stellar Physics

Neutral Gas in Galaxies

January 2022 - June 2022 • Advisor: John Cannon
Performed sophisticated data reduction using CASA software and comprehensive analysis on nearby, star-forming galaxies observed with VLT. Conducted the first spatially-resolved study of neutral gas components, measuring detailed properties and kinematics.
Radio Astronomy CASA Galaxy Evolution

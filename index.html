About Me

I'm a PhD student in Physics at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, where I'm fortunate to be supported by the Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS) and Redbird Scholarship.


My research focuses on applying machine learning techniques to astrophysical problems, particularly in stellar evolution and nucleosynthesis. I'm passionate about bridging the gap between computational methods and fundamental physics, using tools like neural networks to better understand the universe around us.


With a strong background in both theoretical physics and programming, I enjoy tackling complex problems that require innovative computational approaches.

Research Experience

Neural Network Application for Jet Calibration

January 2024 - Current • Advisor: Jennifer Roloff
Developing Machine Learning models for jet calibration, integrating Particle Flow Networks (PFN) for reconstructing jet constituents. Implemented innovative dual-stream framework and knowledge distillation algorithms, achieving improved calibration accuracy with minimal error rates.

Optical Galaxy Redshift Survey for Hydrogen Intensity Mapping

October 2023 - Current • Tianlai Collaboration
Conducted redshift survey in the North Celestial Cap, obtaining 724 redshifts from 2801 targets. Utilized V-band photometry and spectroscopic observations with the WIYN telescope to support hydrogen intensity mapping studies.

Metallicity-Dependent Isotopic Abundance Models

January 2022 - September 2022 • Advisor: Christopher West
Applied machine learning algorithms (Random Forest) and Grid Search to select best-fit isotopic scaling models based on solar observations. Achieved consistent results with less than 8.7% error and motivated modifications to chemical evolution models.

Spatially-resolved Study of Neutral Gas in Galaxies

January 2022 - June 2022 • Advisor: John Cannon
Performed data reduction using CASA and analysis on nearby, star-forming galaxies observed with VLT. Conducted the first spatially-resolved study of neutral gas components, measuring properties and kinematics of these systems.
